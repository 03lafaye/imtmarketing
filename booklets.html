Customized Booklets
For those special listings when a feature sheet just isn’t enough, “WOW” your clients (and the people they will show it to!) with a custom designed booklet. Using a booklet will allow you to showcase every stunning feature of the property in a clean and professional manner. In addition to the benefits to the client, booklets are also an effective marketing tool to use in your listing presentations, setting you apart from other potential agents.
