Feature Sheets
All of our feature sheets are custom designed, giving you a unique product that will impress any client. A small and cost effective investment that will prove to your client that you are going the extra mile. A good quality feature sheet will ensure that your listing is never forgotten. Buyer's are seeing many properties at any given time when shopping for a home, and can often times have trouble distinguishing between homes at the end of their showings. Give them something they can take with them that will ensure they don't forget about you or your listing. Your clients will likely keep a few after closing, or pass them out to friends. A small investment for a powerful marketing tool!

Beautiful Single Page Feature Sheets

Your clients will love our single page feature sheets. They are printed on high-quality paper and designed specifically to correspond with your current or future marketing goals. As with all of our designs, they are high customizable and a cost-effective way to impress your clients.

- Printed on 8.5" x 11" paper

- Choice of high quality 12pt stock or 100 lb. glossy finish

- Turnaround time: 1-2 business days

Elegant Folding Feature Sheets

Impress your clients with our 11" x 17" vertical folding feature sheets. Printed on the same high-quality materials and designed specifically for you. This option allows for larger images and more information for homes that require a little more attention.

- Printed on 11" x 17" paper

- Choice of high quality 12pt stock or 100 lb. glossy finish

- Scored vertically down the centre for a seamless fold

- Turnaround time: 1-2 business days

Unique Calendar Style Feature Sheets

Our calendar style feature sheets are printed on 11" x 17" paper and fold horizontally for a very unique look. They are great for emphasizing the photos of the home and still allow enough space for descriptions, school information, floorplans and more.

- Printed on 11" x 17" paper

- Choice of high quality 12pt stock or 100 lb. glossy finish

- Scored horizontally across the centre for a seamless fold

- Turnaround time: 1-2 business days
